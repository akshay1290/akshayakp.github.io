Mall Customer Segmentation

Project Overview

Customer Segmentation is a popular application of unsupervised learning. Using clustering, identify segments of customers to target the potential user base. They divide customers into groups according to common characteristics like gender, age, interests, and spending habits so they can market to each group effectively. Use K-means clustering and also visualize the gender and age distributions. Then analyze their annual incomes and spending scores.
The project involves the following key steps:
1. Data Collection: Gather data on customers, including their gender, age, annual incomes, and spending scores. This dataset can be obtained from various sources, such as customer records or surveys.
2. Data Preprocessing:Clean and preprocess the data, handling missing values and outliers if necessary. Ensure the dataset is ready for analysis.
3. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA): Conduct an EDA to understand the distribution of gender and age among customers. Visualize the data to gain insights into customer demographics.
4.K-means Clustering: Apply the K-means clustering algorithm to group customers into segments based on their annual incomes and spending scores. Experiment with different numbers of clusters (K) to find the optimal segmentation.
5. Cluster Visualization: Visualize the resulting customer segments, possibly using scatter plots or other visualizations, to understand the characteristics of each segment.
6. Segment Analysis: Analyze each customer segment to identify common traits and behaviors. This analysis can help in tailoring marketing strategies for each segment.

About the dataset

The dataset used in this project contains information about customers and includes the following attributes:
Gender: The gender of the customer (e.g., Male or Female).
Age: The age of the customer.
Annual Income: The annual income of the customer.
Spending Score: A score that quantifies the customer's spending habits and purchasing behavior.

Expected Outcomes

The expected outcomes of this project include:
1. Customer Segmentation: Clear identification of customer segments based on their annual incomes and spending scores.
2. Segment Profiles: Detailed profiles of each customer segment, including characteristics like age, gender, and other shared traits.
3.Insights for Marketing: Insights into the preferences and behaviors of each customer segment, enabling more targeted and effective marketing campaigns.
4.Visualization: Visual representations of customer segments and their characteristics to facilitate decision-making.
